Case Study

$250K at-risk.
Closed at $380K.

I inherited a government account that was a mess. The prior CSM had gone quiet, support tickets were unresolved, and the main admin and business analyst had both left — taking their platform knowledge with them. The customer was frustrated and disengaged. Renewal was in serious doubt.

I reset the relationship from scratch. Held a working session to understand where they actually were, cleared every outstanding support issue, and arranged a complimentary 10-hour dedicated training block with our consulting team — recorded so any future admin could use it. The training became a reusable onboarding resource for the whole account.

Adoption recovered. The relationship stabilized. And in the process, I found a new business unit that hadn't been part of the original scope. What started as a retention problem turned into a growth conversation.
